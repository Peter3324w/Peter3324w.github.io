GUIDE TO GET STARTED | STUDENT

How do I Pay (School Fee/Exam Fee/Other Fees)?

You can pay your fees through the student portal by navigating to the Finance section, or visit the Accounts office for manual payments.

How do I register for Exam?

Navigate to Registration > Exam Registration from the main menu. Complete the required fields and submit your exam registration before the deadline.

How do I register for Termly or Semester Registration?

Go to Registration > Term Registration from the main menu. Complete all required information and submit. Your registration slip will be available for download after successful registration.

How do I fill in an Agreement form.?

Agreement forms can be downloaded from the portal under the Forms section. Fill in the required details and submit to the appropriate office.

How do I Register for First Year

First year registration is done during orientation week. Visit the Registration office with your admission letter and all required documents.
